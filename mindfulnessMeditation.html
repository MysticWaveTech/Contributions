Mindfulness and Meditation

Enhance Your Connection and Awareness

1. Find a Quiet Space

Select a location where you can sit comfortably without distractions.

A quiet room or a peaceful outdoor spot works well.

2. Settle Into Your Position

Sit in a comfortable position, either on a chair or on the floor.

Try these positions:

  • Sitting cross-legged on the floor.
  • Sitting on a chair with your feet flat on the ground.
  • Standing with your weight evenly distributed.

3. Focus on Your Breath

Take deep breaths and pay attention to your inhalation and exhalation.

Feel your chest and abdomen rise and fall with each breath.

4. Observe Your Thoughts

Notice any thoughts that arise without judgment; simply let them pass.

Return your focus to your breath when your mind wanders.

Habit Building

Establish Consistency in Your Practice

1. Identify Key Habits

Choose habits that align with your goals for energy work.

Focus on habits that will contribute to your energy and emotional health.

2. Create a Routine

Incorporate your key habits into a daily routine.

Consider these strategies:

  • Schedule your practices at the same time every day.
  • Use reminders or apps to help you stay on track.
  • Start small and gradually increase your commitment.

3. Track Your Progress

Keep a journal or log to monitor your habit formation.

Celebrate small victories to reinforce your new habits!

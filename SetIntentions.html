Step 1: Set Intentions

Clarify Your Goals and Write Down Your Wishes

Why Setting Intentions is Important

Setting intentions helps you focus your energy and align your actions with your desires. It serves as a roadmap for your personal growth and transformation.

Instructions

Take a moment to reflect on what you truly want in life. Write down your intentions clearly and with purpose. Ensure they are specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART).

Examples of Intentions

  • “I intend to improve my emotional intelligence by practicing mindfulness daily.”
  • “I wish to enhance my intuitive abilities by journaling my dreams every night.”
  • “I aim to manifest financial abundance by actively saving a portion of my income each month.”

Affirmation to Reinforce Your Intentions

Repeat the following affirmation to strengthen your commitment:

“I am aligned with my highest self, and I manifest my intentions with clarity and purpose.”

Visualize Your Intentions

Close your eyes and visualize your intentions coming to life. Imagine how you will feel once you achieve them. Use all your senses to create a vivid mental picture.

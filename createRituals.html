Create Your Rituals

Focus Your Intent

1. Identify Your Intent

Determine the specific outcome you desire from your ritual.

Write down your intention clearly and concisely.

2. Choose Your Tools

Select items that resonate with your intention (candles, crystals, herbs, etc.).

Examples:

  • Green candle for abundance
  • Rose quartz for love
  • Sage for cleansing

3. Set the Scene

Create a dedicated space free from distractions. Arrange your tools mindfully.

4. Perform the Ritual

Engage with your tools, recite affirmations, and visualize your intention.

Take your time and connect with the energy of your ritual.

5. Reflect and Close

After the ritual, take a moment to reflect on your experience.

Close the space by thanking any energies or entities you invited.
